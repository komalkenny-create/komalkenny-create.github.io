Decanter Centrifuge

Professional Solids Control Equipment

The Decanter Centrifuge is the final and finest stage of solids control, separating ultra-fine drilled solids from drilling fluid using high centrifugal force. It's essential for maintaining optimal mud properties in demanding drilling applications and for drilling waste management operations.

CHINA KOMAL centrifuges offer premium construction with dual-phase stainless steel bowls, tungsten carbide protection, and precision bearings for reliable high-speed operation.

Key Features

  • Premium Bowl Construction - Dual-phase stainless steel 2205/2304 centrifugal casting (Premium version)
  • Economic Option - SS304 centrifugal casting bowl for cost-effective solutions
  • Tungsten Carbide Protection - Scroll and discharge ports protected for extended wear life
  • Precision Bearings - SKF/FAG/NKS genuine bearings for reliable high-speed operation
  • High G-Force - Up to 3066G for efficient fine solids separation (2-15μm)
  • VFD Control - Variable frequency drive for precise speed control (optional)
  • Overload Protection - Automatic protection system for motor and gearbox
  • Drive Options - Planetary gearbox or hydraulic motor drive available

Applications

  • Weighted mud systems - Barite recovery and fluid conservation
  • Deep well drilling - Ultra-fine solids removal
  • Horizontal drilling - Extended reach and complex wells
  • Drilling waste management - Solids control and disposal
