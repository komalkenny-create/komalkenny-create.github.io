Desilter

Fine Solids Removal Equipment

The Desilter uses hydro-cyclone technology to remove fine silt-sized particles (15-47μm) from drilling fluid. Positioned after the desander in the solids control sequence, it provides secondary solids removal to maintain optimal drilling fluid properties and reduce abrasive wear on downstream equipment.

Technical Features

  • Hydro-Cyclone Technology - Efficient separation of fine particles (15-47μm)
  • Flexible Configuration - Available with or without underflow shaker
  • Polyurethane Cones - 4 inch (100mm) cones for extended wear life
  • Working Pressure - 0.25-0.4 Mpa optimal operating range
  • Multiple Cone Configurations - 8, 12, or 16 cones based on capacity requirements
  • Easy Maintenance - Simple assembly and disassembly design
