3D2-SZ Triplex Plunger Pump

High Pressure Industrial Pump - 40-170 L/min Flow Rate

The 3D2-SZ is a high-pressure triplex plunger pump with splash or forced lubrication system. This pump features compact structure, stable driving, low friction coefficient, and low temperature rise, making it ideal for long-time continuous operation. The pump can transport water at 0-50°C, emulsion, and various chemical liquids, suitable for coal mining, metallurgy, ship cleaning, and oil field applications.
