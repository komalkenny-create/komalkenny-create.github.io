Mixing Hopper

Professional Solids Control Equipment

CHINA KOMAL mixing hopper is professional solids control equipment designed for oil and gas drilling operations. Featuring high-quality construction and reliable performance, it provides efficient solids control for drilling fluid systems in demanding drilling conditions.

Available Models

KLD-150S

Import Displacement: 240 m³/h

Solid Phase Mixing: Barite 14000kg/h, Bentonite 14968kg/h

KLD1-2

Import Displacement: 180 m³/h

Solid Phase Mixing: Barite 300kg/h, Bentonite 150kg/h

Technical Features

  • Equivalent to SWACO Hiride Eductor - KLD-150S integrates proprietary minimum pressure drop (MPD) nozzle and unique trimix diffuser (TMD)
  • Stainless steel frame - Rugged construction with optimal height for improved ergonomics
  • Wear resistant imported PU material - Extended service life
  • Rapid complete mixing - Dry or liquid additives into fluids system
  • Lowest comparable pressure loss - Efficient operation
  • High shear rates - Creates high shear rates in circulating loop
  • Compact design - Smaller footprint, ideal for rigs where space is at premium
  • Dust free mixing - Dry product mixing without dust
  • Minimize fish eyes - In hard to mix products
  • Flexible design - Eliminates extra equipment
  • Greater worker safety - Reduced exposure to chemicals
