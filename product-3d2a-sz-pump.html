3D2A-SZ Improved Triplex Plunger Pump

Enhanced Design - 8-80 MPa Pressure Range

The 3D2A-SZ is an improved version of the 3D2 pump, featuring advanced needle bearing structure between crankshaft and connecting rod. This design provides lower friction coefficient, excellent lubrication, stable driving, and low temperature rise, making it ideal for long-time continuous operation. The pump can transport water at 0-50°C, emulsion, and various chemical liquids.
