Mud Pump - F Series & 3NB Series

Professional Drilling Mud Pump

CHINA KOMAL offers comprehensive mud pump solutions including F Series and 3NB Series triplex single-acting piston pumps. With power ratings from 500HP to 2200HP, our mud pumps are designed for oil and gas drilling operations worldwide.

We maintain the biggest pump parts warehouse in China, ensuring quick delivery of complete mud pumps and all spare parts including power end components, fluid end components, drive systems, sealing parts, and accessories.

F Series Mud Pump - Design Features

  • Longer Stroke Design - Can be used under lower punching number to prolong use life of sensitive parts in fluid end
  • Combination Lubrication - Forced lubrication and spray lubrication ensures more effective lubrication
  • Durable & Compact - Advanced structure improves pump's stable operation for middle & deep drilling works
  • Interchangeable Parts - Injection valve and bleed valve can change mutually, three cylinders interchangeable
  • Durable Fluid End - Greatly improves performance of triplex mud pump, convenient examination and maintenance

3NB Series Mud Pump - Design Features

  • Power Point - Plate welding structure eliminates internal stress, involute gear transmission for stable operation
  • Spray Lubrication - Crank is hollow complete cast with spray lubrication system
  • Valve Hole Design - Bottom has steps to prevent clack seat from falling
  • Independent Spray Pump - Piston and liner cooled and lubricated by independent spray pump equipment
  • Quick Installation - Piston and in-between beam connected by lathe dog for rapid installing and moving
  • ASA&API Compliant - Injection and outlet flange accords with ASA&API regulations
  • High Commonality - Sensitive parts has strong interchangeability
