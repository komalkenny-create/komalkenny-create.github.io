3D3-SZ K20000 High Pressure Pump

Ultra Large Flow High Pressure Pump - 200KW

The 3D3-SZ (K20000) is an ultra-large flow high pressure plunger pump with technology imported from KAMAT of Germany. With a maximum power of 200KW, this pump is designed for metallurgy scale removal, secondary and tertiary oil extraction, water injection, and other industrial applications requiring extremely large flow rates. It handles both clear water and chemical liquids with exceptional performance.

Applications

  • Oil Field: Secondary and tertiary oil extraction
  • Coal Excavation: High pressure water jetting for coal mining
  • Surface Cleaning: Large-scale excavation equipment cleaning
  • Metallurgy: Scale removal (squama elimination)
  • Water Injection: Enhanced oil recovery operations
  • Industrial Cleaning: Large flow cleaning applications
