Screw Conveyor

Efficient Drill Cuttings Transport System

The screw conveyor is an essential component of the solids control system, designed to efficiently transport drill cuttings discharged from the shale shaker to the next processing stage or disposal area. Featuring a rotating helical screw blade inside a trough, this reliable conveying system ensures continuous and efficient cuttings removal, maintaining optimal drilling fluid cleanliness and rig floor operations.
