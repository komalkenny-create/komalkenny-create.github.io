Piston Rod

The piston rod is a key component connecting the power end and fluid end of the mud pump. Forged from alloy steel and heat-treated for high strength, with multiple surface treatment options including blackening, zinc plating, nickel plating, or chrome plating to meet different customer requirements.

Product Specifications

Specification Details

Surface TreatmentBlackening / Zinc Plating / Nickel Plating / Chrome Plating

Key Features

  • Forged Construction - Alloy steel with heat treatment for high strength
  • Multiple Surface Options - Blackening, zinc, nickel, or chrome plating available
  • High Durability - Designed for demanding drilling conditions
  • Power Transmission - Large end connects via clamp to transmit power
  • Compatible Brands - EMSCO, NATIONAL, OILWELL, IDECO, GARDNER DENVER

Applications

Piston rods are used in mud pumps from major manufacturers including EMSCO, NATIONAL, OILWELL, IDECO, GARDNER DENVER, and various Chinese mud pump models. Suitable for F Series, 3NB Series, and other drilling rig applications.
