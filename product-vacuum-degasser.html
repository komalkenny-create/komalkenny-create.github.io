Vacuum Degasser

Professional Solids Control Equipment

CHINA KOMAL vacuum degasser is professional solids control equipment designed for oil and gas drilling operations. Featuring high-quality construction and reliable performance, it provides efficient solids control for drilling fluid systems in demanding drilling conditions.

Technical Features

  • Vacuum tank operation - Drilling fluid enters with help of vacuum pump, gas leaves tank
  • Constant temperature operation - Water circulation vacuum pump suitable for flammable and explosive gas
  • High-speed spray separation - Drilling fluid sprayed through rotor window at high speed to tank wall
  • Complete gas separation - Gas in drilling mud is broken and completely separated
  • Low center of gravity - Main motor installed at one end to reduce center of gravity
  • Belt drive design - Simplifies reducer structure
  • Gas-liquid separator - Gas and water cannot be released at same time, uninterrupted trachea
  • Water saving - Circulating water supplied to vacuum pump
  • Dual function - Suction pipe can be used as high power mixer when mud doesn't contain gas
