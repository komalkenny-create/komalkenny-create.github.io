KM37 High Pressure Plunger Pump

Professional Fluid Power Equipment

The KM37 series high pressure plunger pump is a professional fluid power equipment manufactured by CHINA KOMAL INTERNATIONAL CO., LTD. This pump features advanced plunger pump design with input speed of 1000r/min and pump speed of 1000r/min.

With compact structure, high efficiency and reliable performance, it is widely used in petroleum, chemical, shipbuilding, electric power and other industries. The pump provides stable high pressure fluid transmission solutions for various industrial applications.
