Truck Mounted Drilling Rig

Professional Drilling Equipment

The Truck Mounted Drilling Rig is a mobile drilling solution featuring an optimum structure with high-level integration, requiring minimal working space. Built on heavy-duty self-propelled chassis available in various drive configurations (8×6, 10×8, 12×8, 14×8, 14×12), it ensures excellent drilling accessibility, cruise capability, and lateral stability.

Key Features

  • Optimum structure - High-level integration, requires small working space
  • Heavy-duty chassis - 8×6, 10×8, 12×8, 14×8, 14×12 drive ways available
  • Hydraulic steering - Ensures good drilling accessibility and lateral stability
  • Engine matching - High driving efficiency and working reliability
  • Drawworks drive - Can be driven by motors
  • Main brake - Band brake or hydraulic disc brake
  • Auxiliary brake - Water cooling thrust plate pneumatic brake or water brake
  • Rotary table - Forward-reverse shift for DP make up/break out
  • Mast - Front-open double-section, hydraulically raised/laid down
  • Drill floor - Twin-body telescopic or parallelogram structure
  • Configurable systems - Solid control, well control, high-pressure manifold
