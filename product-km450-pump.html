KM450 High Pressure Plunger Pump

Professional Fluid Power Equipment

The KM450 series high pressure plunger pump is a professional fluid power equipment manufactured by CHINA KOMAL INTERNATIONAL CO., LTD. This pump features advanced plunger pump design with input speed of 1480r/min (Motor) or 1800r/min (Diesel Engine) and pump speed of 422r/min / 461r/min.

With robust construction, high efficiency and reliable performance, it is widely used in petroleum, chemical, shipbuilding, electric power and other industries. The KM450 series provides multiple configuration options with power ratings from 355kW to 575kW for both motor and diesel engine drive.
