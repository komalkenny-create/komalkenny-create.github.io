Desander

Professional Solids Control Equipment

The Desander is a hydro-cyclone based solids control device that removes sand-sized particles (47-75μm) from drilling fluid. It operates at 0.25-0.4 Mpa working pressure and can be configured with or without an underflow shaker for enhanced solids removal.

Available Models

KPNA Series (Without Underflow Shaker)

Models: KPNA-1S, KPNA-2S, KPNA-3S

Capacity: 120-360 m³/h (500-1500 gpm)

KPMC635 Series (With Underflow Shaker)

Models: KPMC635-1S, KPMC635-2S, KPMC635-3S

Capacity: 120-360 m³/h (500-1500 gpm)

Technical Features

  • Hydro-Cyclone Technology - Efficient separation of sand-sized particles (47-75μm)
  • Flexible Configuration - Available with or without underflow shaker
  • Polyurethane Cones - For extended wear life and corrosion resistance
  • Working Pressure - 0.25-0.4 Mpa optimal operating range
  • Large Capacity - Up to 360 m³/h (1500 gpm) processing capacity
  • Easy Maintenance - Simple assembly and disassembly design
