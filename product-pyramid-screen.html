Pyramid Screen (Wave Type) - 120-150% Increased Filtering Area

CHINA KOMAL pyramid screens feature a unique wave-type design that increases the filtering area by 120% to 150% compared to flat screens. Constructed with 2 or 3 layers of 304 or 316 stainless steel wire cloth compounded with steel backing plates, these screens provide superior solids control performance.

The wire cloth and bottom steel backing plate are tightly bonded to form a wave pattern, creating independent small surfaces that prevent excessive expansion damage and extend working life. Available in hexagonal, rectangular, and square backing plate patterns to suit different shaker models and applications.
