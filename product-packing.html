Packing Materials - Bulk & Structured Packing

CHINA KOMAL offers comprehensive packing materials including bulk packing and structured packing in both metal and plastic materials. Our products feature high flux, small resistance, easy maintenance, and high separation efficiency for various industrial applications.

Bulk packing products include pall rings, raschig rings, CMR, step rings, conjugate rings, Taylor garland, hollow polyhedral balls, floating balls, and O-type packing. Structured packing includes metal orifice, metal thorns orifice plate, sheet metal net (mesh), metal wire mesh, plastic orifice, and grille (gallitch) types.
