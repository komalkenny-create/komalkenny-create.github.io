Horizontal Agitator

Professional Solids Control Equipment

CHINA KOMAL horizontal agitator is professional solids control equipment designed for oil and gas drilling operations. Featuring high-quality construction and reliable performance, it provides efficient solids control for drilling fluid systems in demanding drilling conditions.

Technical Features

  • Circular arc cylindrical reducer - Circular arc worm with cylindrical shape along axis, reliable and effective
  • Simple maintenance - Combination of reducer and explosion-proof engine meets complicated working condition requirements
  • Strong performance - High mixing efficiency in slurry environment with large operation area and high mud viscosity
  • Quick installation - Easy operation and easy maintenance
  • Unique agitation efficiency - Used in large and deep mud tanks
