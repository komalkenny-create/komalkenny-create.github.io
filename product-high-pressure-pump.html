Industrial High-Pressure Pumps

CHINA KOMAL offers a comprehensive range of high-pressure pumps designed for demanding oilfield, water jetting, and industrial applications. Our pumps deliver reliable performance, extended service life, and optimal efficiency in the most challenging environments.

Triplex Pump

High-pressure triplex plunger pumps for water jetting, oilfield washing, and pressure testing applications. Compact design with excellent pressure stability.

Quintuplex Pump

Five-cylinder pumps for ultra-high pressure applications. Smoother flow, reduced vibration, and higher efficiency compared to triplex designs.

Water Jet Pump

Specialized pumps for hydro-blasting, surface preparation, and industrial cleaning. Pressures up to 40,000 PSI available.

Fracturing Pump

Heavy-duty pumps for hydraulic fracturing operations. Built to withstand extreme pressures and abrasive fracturing fluids.

Cementing Pump

High-pressure pumps for well cementing services. Precise flow control and reliable performance for primary and remedial cementing.

Key Features

  • Pressure ratings from 5,000 to 40,000 PSI
  • Forged steel fluid ends for maximum durability
  • Advanced sealing technology for leak-free operation
  • Easy maintenance access and quick-change parts
  • API 674 and API 675 compliant designs
  • Customizable configurations for specific applications
  • Global after-sales support and spare parts availability

Applications

  • Oil & Gas Well Drilling and Completion
  • Hydraulic Fracturing (Fracking)
  • Well Cementing Services
  • Water Jet Cutting and Cleaning
  • Surface Preparation and Descaling
  • High-Pressure Water Testing
  • Chemical Injection Systems
  • Industrial High-Pressure Cleaning
