Mud Cleaner

3-in-1 Solids Control Unit

The Mud Cleaner is a compact 3-in-1 unit combining Desander, Desilter, and Shale Shaker for efficient solids control. It provides multi-stage separation to remove drilled cuttings from drilling fluid, maintaining optimal mud properties while minimizing fluid loss.

CHINA KOMAL mud cleaners feature polyurethane hydro-cyclones for extended life, adjustable shaker deck angles, and premium electrical components for reliable operation in demanding drilling conditions.

Available Models

KQJ Series (Standard Mud Cleaner)

Models: KQJ-1B, KQJ-2S, KQJ-3D

Capacity: 240 m³/h (1056 gpm)

KPMC Series (Compact Mud Cleaner)

Models: KPMC-1S8N, KPMC-2S12N, KPMC-3S16N

Capacity: 120-360 m³/h (500-1500 gpm)

Technical Features

  • 3-in-1 Compact Design - Desander & Desilter & Shale Shaker integrated unit for compact footprint
  • Polyurethane Hydro-Cyclones - Inside for long life, metal outside for easy assembly and disassembly
  • Adjustable Deck Angle - Shaker deck angle adjustable while working, mechanical adjustment with reliable performance
  • Stainless Steel Bottom Deck - For long service life
  • Heat Treatment - On complete shaker deck for High G force operation
  • Pretension Shaker Screen - For fast replacement
  • Premium Vibration Motors - IEC Ex, ATEX and UL Certified available
  • Premium Electrical Components - SIEMENS, Schneider or ABB available
