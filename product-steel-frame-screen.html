Steel Frame Screen - High Strength Construction

CHINA KOMAL steel frame screens feature 2 or 3 layers of 304 or 316 stainless steel wire cloth combined with a high-strength steel frame and backing plate. This robust construction provides exceptional durability and filtering performance for demanding drilling applications.

The bottom high-strength steel frame with supporting bars works together with moderately tensioned screen cloth to infinitely enhance screen intensity and endurance. Mesh sizes ranging from API 20 to 325 are available, with independent small surfaces that prevent excessive expansion damage. Special rubber plugs allow for quick damage repair, saving replacement time and reducing operational costs.
