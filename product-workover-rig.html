Workover Rig

Professional Drilling Equipment

The Workover Rig is designed for well maintenance, repair, and workover operations with a reasonable structure and high degree of mechanization. It features a small floor area with efficient hydraulic and mechanical transmission systems.

Key Features

  • Reasonable structure - High degree of mechanization, small floor area
  • Transmission - Hydraulic and mechanical, high efficiency
  • Chassis options - Special chassis or self-propelled chassis
  • Derrick - Single/double opening, hydraulic lift
  • Brake system - Band/hydraulic disc brake + pneumatic water brake
  • Floor - Two body telescopic or parallelogram structure
  • Customizable - Table size and height per requirements
