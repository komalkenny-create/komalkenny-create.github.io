Screw Pump

Professional Solids Control Equipment

CHINA KOMAL screw pump is professional solids control equipment designed for oil and gas drilling operations. Featuring high-quality construction and reliable performance, it provides efficient solids control for drilling fluid systems in demanding drilling conditions.

Available Models

KPG Series Single Screw Pump

Models: KPG10A-040, KPG20A-055, KPG30A-075, KPG40A-110, KPG50A-110, KPG60A-150, KPG70A-220, KPG80A-220

Capacity: 10-80 m³/h (44-352 gpm)

Pressure: 0.3 MPa

Technical Features

  • Centrifuge feed pump drive options - Coupler, variable speed motor, triangle V-belt, gearbox
  • Compact structure - Less accessories, small volume, easy maintenance
  • Replaceable wear parts - Rotor and stator are convenient to replace
  • Elastomeric stator material - Particular advantages for high viscosity fluids with hard suspended particles
  • Stable linear flow - No valves installation required compared with centrifugal pump
  • Versatile applications - Can transport mixed impurities containing gas, solid particles, fibers, and corrosive substances
  • High viscosity capability - Can transport high viscosity material compared with gear pump
  • Precision metering - Can be used for pharmaceutical filling and metering compared with plunger/diaphragm pump
