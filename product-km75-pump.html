Professional Fluid Power Equipment

The KM75 series high pressure plunger pump is a professional fluid power equipment manufactured by CHINA KOMAL INTERNATIONAL CO., LTD. This pump features advanced plunger pump design with input speed of 1000r/min and pump speed of 1000r/min.
