Wire-Wrapped Screen - T-Shape Self-Cleaning Design

CHINA KOMAL wire-wrapped screens feature a T-shape self-cleaning design that minimizes blocking and allows for easy reverse washing. With acid and alkali resistance, these screens provide long service life in demanding well conditions.

The minimum slot width reaches 0.1mm, satisfying most sand control requirements. With large flow area reaching at least 10%, these screens are ideal for sand control operations in vertical wells, deviated wells, directional wells, and especially preference for strong acid and alkaline well conditions with various corrosive media.
