Stainless Steel Wire Mesh for Shaker Screen

CHINA KOMAL stainless steel wire mesh is manufactured from high-quality SUS302, 304, 304L, 316, 316L, and 310S materials. Available in 19 specifications from Mesh 2 to Mesh 325, our wire mesh features plain weave, twill weave, and bamboo flowers weaving patterns for various filtration applications.

With excellent acid resistance, alkali resistance, heat resistance, and wear resistance properties, our stainless steel wire mesh is widely used in mining, chemical, food, petroleum, and medicine industries for gas and liquid filtration and media separation.
