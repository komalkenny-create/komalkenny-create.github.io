3D3-SZ High Pressure Pump

Large Flow High Pressure Pump - German KAMAT Technology

The 3D3-SZ (K2000) is a high pressure plunger pump with large flow capacity, imported from KAMAT of Germany. With a maximum power of 200KW, this pump is specifically designed for metallurgy scale removal, secondary and tertiary oil extraction, water injection, and other industrial applications requiring large flow rates. It can handle both clear water and various chemical liquids with exceptional reliability.

Applications

  • Metallurgy: Scale removal (squama elimination) in metal processing
  • Oil Extraction: Secondary oil recovery operations
  • Tertiary Oil Recovery: Water injection (affusion) for enhanced oil recovery
  • Water Injection: Industrial water injection applications
  • Chemical Industry: Chemical liquid transmission and processing
  • Large Flow Applications: Various industrial fields requiring large flow rates
