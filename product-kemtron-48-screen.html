Kemtron KTL 48 Shaker Screen

Premium Replacement Solids Control Equipment

The Kemtron KTL 48 shaker screen is a high-performance replacement screen designed for KEMTRON KTL 48 series shale shakers. Featuring Q235 steel frame and SS 304/316 wire mesh cloth construction, these screens deliver superior solids control efficiency and extended service life for demanding drilling operations.

Manufactured with premium stainless steel 304/316/316L materials, compliant with API RP 13C standards, KTL 48 screens are available in API 20 to API 230 mesh designations. Compatible with KEMTRON KTL 48 shale shaker, KTL-448 AG four panel shaker, KTL-48 STD/D/SS three panel shakers, and KEMTRON 48 series desander/desilter systems.
