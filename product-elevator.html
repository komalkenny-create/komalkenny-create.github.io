Drilling Elevator

Premium Quality Drilling Equipment

The Elevator is an essential tool used on the drilling rig floor to grip and lift drill pipe, casing, or tubing during tripping operations. It latches around the pipe body or tool joint and connects to the hook or traveling block, enabling safe and efficient handling of tubular goods.

CHINA KOMAL elevators are manufactured to the highest quality standards, featuring robust construction and reliable locking mechanisms for safe pipe handling.

Key Features

  • Forged Steel Construction - Premium alloy steel for exceptional strength
  • Reliable Locking Mechanism - Secure latch system prevents accidental opening
  • Wear-Resistant Inserts - Replaceable dies for extended service life
  • Ergonomic Design - Easy operation for rig floor crew
  • Multiple Pipe Sizes - Available for various pipe and casing diameters
  • Safety Indicators - Visual confirmation of proper latch engagement
  • Corrosion Protection - Advanced coating for harsh environments
  • API Certified - Meets API 8C and ISO standards
