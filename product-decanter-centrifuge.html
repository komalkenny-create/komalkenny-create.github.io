Decanter Centrifuge

High-Speed Solids Control Equipment

The Decanter Centrifuge is the final and finest stage of solids control, separating ultra-fine particles (2-15μm) from drilling fluid using centrifugal force up to 3066G. It provides precise control of drilling fluid properties and is essential for maintaining optimal mud weight and viscosity in demanding drilling operations.

CHINA KOMAL offers both Premium and Economic series centrifuges with fixed speed, VFD, and hydraulic drive options to meet various operational requirements and budget considerations.

Available Models

Premium Series

Models: KPDC-1400F, KPDC-1400V, KPDC-1800V, KPDC-2200V

Bowl Diameter: 14" to 22" (360mm to 550mm)

Max G Force: Up to 3066G

Premium Centrifuge Features

  • Premium Design - Highest configuration and best material for long and reliable service
  • Duplex Stainless Steel Bowl - Cylindrical and Conical section made from 2205/2304 by centrifugal casting
  • SS304/SS316L Components - Other parts of bowl assembly in stainless steel
  • Tungsten Carbide Protection - Screw protection and solids discharge port for extended life
  • Adjustable Weir Height - Easily adjustable liquid discharge weir for flexible application
  • Premium Bearings - Genuine SKF/FAG/NKS bearings for reliable operation
  • Overload Protection - Limit switch protection for fixed speed and VFD centrifuges
  • Planetary Gear Gearbox - For fixed speed and VFD centrifuges; Hydraulic motor option available
