KM315 High Pressure Plunger Pump

Professional Fluid Power Equipment

The KM315 series high pressure plunger pump is a professional fluid power equipment manufactured by CHINA KOMAL INTERNATIONAL CO., LTD. This pump features advanced plunger pump design with input speed of 1480r/min (Motor) or 1800r/min (Diesel Engine) and pump speed of 366r/min.

With robust construction, high efficiency and reliable performance, it is widely used in petroleum, chemical, shipbuilding, electric power and other industries. The KM315 series provides multiple configuration options with power ratings from 160kW to 345kW for both motor and diesel engine drive.
