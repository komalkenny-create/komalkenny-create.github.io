About CHINA KOMAL

Leading Oilfield Equipment Manufacturer

CHINA KOMAL INTERNATIONAL CO., LTD is a professional international enterprise specializing in the R&D, manufacturing and sales of oilfield equipment. Established in 2010, the company is headquartered in Beijing and owns a modern production base and a comprehensive sales network.

Main products include: petroleum equipment, solids control systems, mud pump parts, shaker screens, and high pressure pumps. Our products are widely used in the fields of oil and natural gas exploration and development, and are exported to the Middle East, North America, South America, Southeast Asia and other regions, highly trusted by customers worldwide.

Our Vision: To be the best manufacturer and supplier for oil field equipment globally, delivering premium quality and exceptional service.
