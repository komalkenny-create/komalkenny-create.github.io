Slotted Liner & Drilled-hole Pipe - API RP 13C (ISO 13501) Compliant

CHINA KOMAL slotted liners and drilled-hole pipes are manufactured in strict compliance with API RP 13C (ISO 13501) standards. These products feature burr-free, uniform slots with comprehensive anti-corrosion treatment for extended downhole working life.

The dense protective layer formed on all pipe surfaces enhances corrosion and wear resistance. With large inner diameter, simple operation, and convenient pipe string configuration, these liners are ideal for sand rock and carbonate reservoirs, especially horizontal wells.

Working Principle

Slotted liners and drilled-hole pipes allow certain size sand to come through the surface with crude oil while keeping bigger size sand outside to form a sand bridge, thereby achieving the anti-sand purpose. This type of product is commonly used in sand rock and carbonate reservoirs, and is especially suitable for horizontal wells.
