Vertical Cutting Dryer

Professional Solids Control Equipment

CHINA KOMAL vertical cutting dryer is professional solids control equipment designed for oil and gas drilling operations. Featuring high-quality construction and reliable performance, it provides efficient solids control for drilling fluid systems in demanding drilling conditions.

KLS-1

Screen Max. Diameter: 700 mm

KLS-2

Max. Treating Capacity: 30-50 t/h

Technical Features

  • Stainless steel rotary parts - Corrosion resistant construction
  • High separation G force - 420G under 900 rpm regular speed, 750G under 1200 rpm with VFD starter
  • HRC65 chilled steel scraper - On rotor, longer service life than competitors
  • Air cutter equipped - Wash water based mud with high viscosity
  • Oil lubrication system - Ensure operation safety of bearings
  • Control panel options - Fixed speed or VFD control panel available
  • Famous brand components - SIEMENS, Schneider or ABB electrical components
  • Certified motors - IEC Ex, ATEX and UL Certificate available
